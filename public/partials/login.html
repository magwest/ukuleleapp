The Bad Ass Ukulele Song Machine


Simply an awesome tool when you want to pick up the Ukulele

Create songs fast by simply drag chords and drop them wherever you want them to be in a song..
Choose between different "voicings" of the chord eg. different ways of how to play the chord
Add comments or notes about the songs

In just a fiew minutes you'll have a visual representation of how you will play the song on the Ukulele.

Also an awesome tool for song writing!

"The Open Source of Ukulele Songs"

Build a song library with all your Ukulele songs you make. Create "playlists" and view several songs from your library in one printable view

Share your songs to other users and browse other users songs aswell
When you find something cool, save it to your own library! And if you want - edit it in the way you want it and share it again..
